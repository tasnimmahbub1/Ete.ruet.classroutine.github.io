LFR WORKSHOP

Date:Wednesday, 10 August 2022
Time:
19-06-2022:LINE FOLLOWER ROBOT WORKSHOP
Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering organized a workshop on Line following robots for all series students. Resource person: Md Abu Ismail Siddique, Lecturer, ETE, RUET gives an overall overview of the mechanical, electrical, and programming parts for line following robot. After that, another key resource person: Shuvra Prokash Biswas, Assistant Professor, ETE, RUET gives an overview of different types of sensors and motors. We are grateful to our Head sir Professor Dr. Md. Kamal Hossain sir for his great initiative for the students.
